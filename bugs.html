PROJECT NAME

Salvaged Entomology

DATE

December 2017

MATERIALS

Physical Computing, Recycled goods

Details

Salvaged Entomology aims to explore the feeling of uneasiness surrounding insects and poor waste management. These two topics plague our everyday lives. In this project, I used materials normally deemed as “trash”to bring attention to the irony of how we treat our world. This project is about nature, recycling and simple organisms. It is influenced by Lauren Singer’s Zero Waste Lifestyle, and simple robot such as WinkBot and Kilobot. I have undertaken the project by investigating robotics, insects-human relationships, and the work of many artists/engineers. I then looked into using recycled materials as primary building materials. This project’s final form is a cross section of artificial life and sustainability and is exhibited as a series of photographs showcasing the artificial organisms in their natural habitat.
