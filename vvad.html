PROJECT NAME

Interaction Design: Adverator

DATE

January 2016

MATERIALS

After Effects, Figma, Leaflet.js

Details

While interning Vaudeville Ventures, I designed, iterated and prototyped new features for a dynamic advertising company, Adverartor---also known as Addy. Addy’s primary objective is to make local market advertising easier and less expensive than traditional means of location based advertising. Prior to Addy, advertisers would have data on what their ideal demographics would be, but often would result in wide and expensive campaigns that may not reach the ideal audience. Addy allows advertisers create ideal campaigns for local markets based on demographics and budget.

Design prompt

Design a touch centric first approach to for customers to create their localized ad campaign. Bonus points for Hi -Fi prototyping and examples of technical feasibility.

RESEARCH + Motivations

Although short and simple, my initial promoted did not address exactly why this was a required feature. So with some digging into the Addy’s analytics revealed that a growing population was visiting Addy though smart phones and tablet. Additionally, 34% of Addy’s new visitors where dropping off during Addy’s selection flow



Direct customer interviews and feedback revealed that customers really liked Addy’s location based solution, but often wanted to target specific demographics within certain locations. It was clear that Addy’s current solution wasn’t very easy to use and didn’t quite meet a customer’s needs

solutions

In my solution, users have a dynamically changing map that refreshes based on selected specifications. Rather than the list that was previously used, my map allows users to visually see where their advertisements can be displayed, and visualize what neighborhoods these locations are located in.
