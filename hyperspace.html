An immersive outerspace VR theater experience


Debuting in at the 2017 Global Cre8 Summit in Shenzhen, Hyperspace aims to redefine how we interact with the people around us and with those beyond our planet in the not-so-distant future.


Hyperspace is a virtual reality experience designed to transport guests into the void of space. As guests enter, they are greeted by a friendly lifeform. Together they traverse neighboring worlds and explore universal conscience.
