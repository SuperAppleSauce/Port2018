PROJECT NAME

Thaw It

DATE

March 2016

MATERIALS

Paper + Pencil, Illustrator, Figma, Marvel

Details

Thaw It is centered around The Internet of Things. The Marvel iframe below is meant to show the evolution of the iterative process for a Smart Fridge app.

Goals

Create an Internet of Things mobile application that allows users to remotely control sections of their freezer.

Research

I conducted user interviews with potential users and found that users were often forgetful. Many of them had trouble remembering shopping lists as well. I also looking into existing fridge apps, mainly Samsung’s Family Hub Touchscreen Fridge. I evaluated that the fridge itself was out of the price range of most of my users. In addition, the fridge had one too many features that could easily confuse users.

Solution

Thaw It helps forgetful users by letting them thaw frozen foods last minute when they aren’t home. Remotely controlling their fridge and having access to shopping lists that are created when users are at home allows users to easily recall forgotten details pertaining to the contents of their fridges. Unfortunately, my fridge app would also require a high tech fridge, and would likely also be costly.
