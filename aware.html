MATERIALS

Problem Statement

How might we solve provide a wellness solution to lawyers who are facing mental health issues?

Situation

Lawyers are 3.6 times more likely to suffer from depression compared to the average population.

32% of lawyers under 30 are problem drinkers.

$800K Yearly cost related to mental health at 1000 lawyer firm

Solution

A wellbeing platform focused on community building and AI guided resilience training.
